Overview

Created for MiniJam 125/MiniJam 125.

MiniJam 125's theme was "signal".

The original concept was to make the use of a radar, it would detect our player and alert enemies to our character's presence.

The limitation of this jam, was "everything is tied to the rhythm". Both Tv's that flash to provide light a heart rate system. It allowed the world and the protagonist to feel connected.

Synopsis/Demo

Static is lost at sea, around them are the horrors of other units like them self, strung up and left to rot.

The other units still have some power and will attempt to light the way for static to reach their home.

The search light will increase Static's heart rate, the TV's will also flicker in time with the heart rate.

Watch out for the creatures of the deep, is this how the others met their end?

Character Mechanics

Movement: The player's main and only real mechanic, it allows static to traverse the level. Using the environment and timing their movement will be key to avoiding detection.

Level Mechanics

Oxygen Cannisters: Unlike most games, the higher your heart rate the closer you are to death. Collecting cannisters will reduce the player's heart rate to a comfotrable level.

Radar: The radar is similar to a lighthouse, if it detects the player it will sound off an alarm.

TV's: These are tied to the player's heartrate and will produce light when nearby this is helpful when navigating through the darkness.

Enemy Mechanics

Cyber-Sharks

CyberSharks will chase the player if they are within a certain radius.
