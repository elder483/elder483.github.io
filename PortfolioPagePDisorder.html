Overview

Developed in Unreal Engine 4/Unreal Engine 4

A prototype/work in progress shooter game, with the intent of having weapons with different firing modes.

These modes are designed to be utilised by the player during combat, traversal as well as finding secrets.

Synopsis/Demo

In this build the player has to navigate the dreamscape, there are four main sections, the tutorial area, the garden, the bridge and tower.

The player has two bosses to defeat and two weapons with four modes to utilise.

Quest was never fully implemented but the fisherman can be spoken to, almost like an NPC providing a bit more information on the world.

The player must make use of both traversal and combat abilities to traverse the world.
