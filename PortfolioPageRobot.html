Overview

Developed in Unreal Engine 4

A week long project, where we were tasked to create a project which would help develop our skillset.

I focused mainly on cameras as I generally had a problem with most projects due to camera issues. I also experimented with box traces + physics. The camera transition screens were inspired by games such as Resident Evil/Biohazard and Devil May Cry.

Responsibilities

Creating all assets

Game & Level Design.

Prototyping and implementing all mechanics

UI/UX Design/implementation.

Synopsis/Demo

The games synopsis, features a robot navigating through a factory, by using his magnet arm.

New Skills Developed
