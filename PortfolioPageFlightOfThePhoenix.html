Overview

Submitted as part of the Game Dev Network Games Jam 2021

This was the first games Jam I did outside of University.

Flight of the Phoenix is an endless runner where the player controls a phoenix flying through a chasm.

As the theme for this GamesJam was Rebirth, we decided to incorporate a rebirth mechanic. When the player gains 10 coins they are able to revive when they crash, In doing so they will be “reborn” and will lose all coins in their current position.

Synopsis/Demo

Flight of the Phoenix is an endless runner where the player controls a phoenix flying through a chasm. With the power of rebirth at your disposal how long will you last.

Character Mechanics

Movement: The player can move both vertically and horizontally, as the game is an endless runner, forward speed will be applied on it's own.

Boost: The player can hold down the boost button to increase the phoenix's speed.

Break: The player can use the break to slow the phoenix down allowing for easier control during tighter situations such as passing through a hole in a wall.

Turn: At the end of a path the player will seemingly come to a dead end, the player should turn to the direction where the path continues.

Fireball: A fireball can be used if the player collects a flame, this can be used to destroy objects in the birds path.

FireDash: The player can use a firedash to pass through an obstacle, acting as a defense mechanism, however it requires good timing to pull off.

Rebirth: Once the player obtains 10 or more coins the player will revive when they get destroyed by an obstacle. They loose all coins when this occurs.

Power Up Mechanics and obstacles

Power Ups

Flames: This is a flame stored in a cone shaped cannister, collecting them allows the phoenix to perform one of two actions, fire dash or fireball.

Feathers: This provides the phoenix a short burst of speed for a limited duration.

Obstacles

Boulders: These boulders can break off from the ceiling of a cavern and bounce, their unpredictability makes them a tough obstacle to dodge.

Volcanic fissures: These fissures will erupt pillars of fire which will destroy the phoenix, it would be wise to steer clear of these.

Cavern walls: These walls with have tiht gaps that require the player to fit within the gaps, or use one of their flame abilities to pass the wall.
