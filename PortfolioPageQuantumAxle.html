Overview

This project was a three person project over the course of a weekend. My primary roles in this project were lead project, designer and developer.
Achieved 1st place in the MixJam9 Submission MixJam9 Results Page
A theme generator was used for the prompts the game had to follow, 'Robots', 'Time Travel' and 'Skateboards'.
Originally based on 'Sonic The Hedgehog 2's Chaos Emerald Mini-game' this game evolved to become an endless runner as it allowed the team to focus more on generating obstacles.
This was my first games jam with Unreal Engine being in the Japanese language.

Problems and Solutions

The Character

The main issue was more about designing the game and what mechanics should the player have.
The theme having skateboards I decided the character needed to have elements that players of skateboard games would come to expect.
This meant developing a point system and a couple of tricks.
The first trick was a combo after performing a jump, re-pressing the button would perform a flip and could be done a few times in succession.
The next was a grind rail, this meant the player could perform a jump, air-trick and then land on a grind rail to rack up points.

Enemies and Obstacles

The enemies and obstacles needed to get the player moving and to provide a challenge.
The first type of enemies were ones that would shoot a projectile and remain stationary.
The second group of enemies will move towards the player and shoot meaning the player would have to react quicker to approaching enemies.
The final was a unqiue enemy the UFO which would pick up the player and the player would have to alternate and press repeatedly the A and D keys to break free.

Story and Level Structure

The story was simply a robot who performed cool tricks which could transcend time and space.
The main issue was how to alternate through time, should they be seperate levels?
Instead I opted for an endless runner, using time portals this could change the upcoming tiles, enemies and visuals.
